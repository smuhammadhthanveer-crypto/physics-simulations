# Physics Simulations

A collection of computational physics and VPython/GlowScript simulations created while learning programming, numerical methods, and physics.

## Current Projects

### 01 - Car Chase Kinematics

A VPython/GlowScript simulation comparing two cars moving under different conditions.

* Car A moves at a constant velocity.
* Car B starts from rest and accelerates uniformly.

The simulation plots position against time and determines when and where the two cars meet.

Users can modify the initial positions, velocities, accelerations, and time step values to explore different kinematics scenarios and observe how the resulting motion changes.

#### Example Results

Using:

* Initial position of Car A = 0.5 m
* Initial position of Car B = 0 m
* Velocity of Car A = 0.45 m/s
* Acceleration of Car B = 0.2 m/s²

The simulation predicts:

* Meeting Position ≈ 2.94 m
* Meeting Time ≈ 5.42 s
